Reservation Policy

1. Deposit & Confirmation

To confirm the event reservation, the client must submit a non-refundable deposit within 72 hours of this proposal being issued. The date will only be blocked upon receipt of the deposit. La Rosa Gastrobar does not guarantee date availability until payment is confirmed.

2. Cancellation by Client

If the client cancels more than 30 days before the event, the deposit will be applied as a credit toward a future event within the next 12 months. If cancellation occurs less than 30 days before the event, the deposit will be forfeited in full. For events with a total value exceeding $2,000, a second payment of 50% of the remaining balance will be required 14 days before the event.

3. Menu & Modifications

The menu agreed upon in this proposal is what will be served on the day of the event. Any modifications must be requested at least 7 days in advance and are subject to availability and possible price adjustments. La Rosa Gastrobar reserves the right to substitute ingredients due to seasonal availability, always maintaining the agreed quality standard.

4. Guest Count

Variations of up to 10% of the confirmed guest count can be accommodated at no extra cost with 48 hours notice. Variations exceeding 10% are subject to proportional price adjustment.

5. Schedule & Duration

The event will have the duration agreed upon in this proposal. Time extensions are available at $300/additional hour, subject to space and staff availability.

6. Responsibilities

The client is responsible for the behavior of their guests within the premises. Any damage to the property will be billed to the client. La Rosa Gastrobar reserves the right to end the event if guest behavior poses a risk to staff or property.
